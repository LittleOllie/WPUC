Drop printable coloring pages here (PDF or PNG).

Then add each file to ../coloring-pages.json — copy an entry from the example block at the bottom of that file.

Suggested naming:
  my-page.pdf              — the printable file
  my-page-thumb.png        — optional preview (square-ish works best)

Paths in coloring-pages.json are relative to the creative-lab/ folder, e.g. "pages/my-page.pdf".
