Grid builder

Build a shareable grid from this wallet’s Quirkies, QuirKid (companion art), Quirklings, and INX.

After you tap Generate grid, please wait up to a minute while tiles load — art is often fetched from IPFS and can be slow.

Some NFTs may not show a preview; metadata and IPFS gateways are not always reliable. We’re aware and working on it.
