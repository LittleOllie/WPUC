Quirks · Ethereum

Welcome

Check Quirkies (with optional QuirkKid art), Quirklings, and INX in one place. Quirklings sort by ID (1–5000 first, then higher IDs).

What you can do

  • Wallet — Scan an address to see pairs, singles, and what you’re missing.
  • Token lookup — Enter one ID to compare Quirkie, QuirkKid, Quirkling, and INX side by side.
  • Grid builder — After a wallet check, arrange a shareable grid, drag tiles, and download.

A quick note from me
