How it works

  1. Paste your post — plain text or text you’ve already styled elsewhere. Emojis stay as they are.
  2. Select what to style — highlight words with your mouse or finger, or tap the line numbers on the left to pick whole lines (shift-tap for multiple lines).
  3. Pick a style — the font list appears when something is selected. Tap a style to apply it. Your selection stays put so you can try different looks.
  4. Copy & post — hit Copy Styled Post, then paste straight into X. The fancy Unicode fonts travel with your text.

Hashtags and @mentions can stay normal using the toggles below the editor. The character counter is an estimate — X may count some styled characters differently.
