The Heart of Little Ollie World

Come Meet
the Family

They’re the heart of every story, adventure and big little lesson, the family who make Little Ollie World feel warm, funny and full of heart.

Tap a character below to meet them. Each one brings their own spark to the stories we’re creating together as a family.

Dad, Mum, Ollie, Lily and Jack are inspired by real family moments, the funny ones, the messy ones and the ones that turn into the best stories.

Each character brings something different to the page. Together they help turn everyday childhood experiences into stories kids can laugh at, learn from and see themselves in.

Stories with a little more inside

Big Little
Lessons

Little Ollie stories are made to be fun, colourful and full of imagination, but each adventure also carries a meaningful little lesson.

Through everyday moments at home, at school and with friends, we explore kindness, courage, empathy, resilience and friendship in relatable adventures.

The lessons are woven naturally into the story, never heavy or forced, so children can enjoy them and talk about them with the grown-ups in their lives.

Our hope is that every book leaves children with a smile, a question to think about, or one small idea they can carry into their day.
